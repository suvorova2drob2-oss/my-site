As a graphic designer I know more than most about losing out to AI as our whole industry was turned upside down by new technology in the 80s with many people being made redundant. You have to hand it to the programmers writing the algorithms and training the AI – the creations are simply incredible by any standard. Many artists seem to fear AI, whereas I'm all for it! In my humble opinion, this is all about artists not wanting to move with the times. Lexical drop

Reading: The robots are taking over

How it works. You see a full sentence with one gap ____. Only 1–4 words from the text fill the gap. If Check is wrong or you are too slow, the correct answer appears in large type and that phrase is saved for a later round. Use the paraphrase hint, then type and press Enter or Check before the line hits the bottom.

Each round you aim for up to ten correct catches; speed is the same for every drop in that round. Rounds 1–4 use the slowest tier, then each new round is a bit faster. Play several rounds until every phrase in this reading has been answered correctly at least once — then you earn a checkmark. Phrases you miss can come back in later rounds.
