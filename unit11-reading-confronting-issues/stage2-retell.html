Stage 2 — Chained retell (all 4 in order)

Speak in sequence: Text 1 → 2 → 3 → 4. Use the key ideas below; you do not have to use every word. Aim for a clear, connected monologue, not a word-for-word read-out.

Устно, подряд: сначала весь смысл первого текста, затем второго и т.д. Опорные фразы — подсказка, а не «зубрёжка».

Confronting issues (hub) · Unit 11

How to run it: one minute’s prep with the key chips (optional) → retell A → short pause → retell B → C → D. If you work in pairs, Partner B repeats the same chain after listening once, or you alternate texts.

A LAND RECLAMATION

Say: why countries need more land, what they do to the sea, one Asian example, then environment vs fishing. Зачем намывают сушу, чем платят экология и рыбаки.

reclaim from the sea artificial islands explosion of projects price to pay fish stocks / livelihoods

B CLOUD SEEDING

Say: the problem (water), what gets sprayed into clouds, why it is in the news, the debate + Wyoming numbers, the downside. Суть посева облаков, спорен ли эффект, плюс цифры и минусы.

water scarcity / shortages whereby (method) press attention open to debate / definitive precipitation 5–15% on the downside
3 / 4

C UNDERWATER FARMING

Say: Noli / Nemo’s Garden, who started it, how the biosphere “works” (water cycle, no pests), future hope for sustainable farming. Про подводные биосферы, воду, вредителей и «нехватку полей».

science fiction vs reality off the coast (Noli) biospheres evaporate → condense → fresh water sustainable / shortage of farmland

D RECYCLING DOG WASTE

Say: the lamp + the bag routine, what happens in the biodigester, tests before 2017, the modest claim (not the energy crisis), other countries’ schemes. Фонарь, метан, испытания, педагогический эффект, распространение идей.

powered by biodigester → methane trials / full operation 2017 put an end to the crisis? (irony) serve to teach schemes adopted (Canada–India)
