Why have the people chosen to go on these different holidays?

Student A — speculate

  • look + adjective — They look very relaxed.
  • look like + noun — They look like tourists.
  • look as if + clause — They look as if they are enjoying themselves.
  • Modal verbs — They might be going to work. / They must be very interested in wildlife.
  • Other verbs — I imagine/expect they like the outdoor life.

Student B — preferences

  • I’d prefer / love / hate to go on a safari.
  • I’d rather do this than that.
  • I’d be nervous about / enjoy riding one of those.
  • I’d be thrilled / scared to travel like that.
