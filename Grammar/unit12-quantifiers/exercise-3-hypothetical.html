Complete the sentences with the correct form of the verb in brackets. Use contracted forms (e.g. didn’t, I’d) where natural, or full forms — both are accepted where equivalent.

1. I wish you with your mouth open. It’s rude! (not eat)

2. It’s time we some regular exercise. (do)

3. If only people to my advice. They never do! (listen)

4. I’d rather Max me when I am busy at work. (not call)

5. Do you wish you to cycle in your childhood? (learn)

6. If only I all that money! I’ve got none left. (not spend)

7. Does Sarah wish she to that amazing musical now? (go)

8. We’d rather you this information private. (keep)
