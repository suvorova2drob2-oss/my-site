Task 2 — Contrast the two pieces (orally)

No writing box on this page. In 1–2 minutes, explain how Text A and Text B fit together: one stresses money and exclusion, the other the value of culture in society. You can use English, Russian, or mix them.

Устно: в чём «боль» первого фрагмента и в чём смысл второго. Необязательно писать — можно продиктовать в заметки на телефон, если удобно. Этого задания достаточно, чтобы «прошла» структура: контраст + пара полезных выражений.

Say at least (loosely)

  • one point about rising costs and who is priced out / bereft of experiences (Text A);
  • one point about why prices rise in the text (funding, production, passed on to end users);
  • one point about the difficulty of defining “culture” but the enormous benefits / enrichment / engagement (Text B);
  • one line to link the two: e.g. if access is a problem, does that reduce the benefits to society, or is that a different question?

Phrases you may echo (if they fit your mouth)

priced out of the financially elite skyrocketed bereft of passed on to end users notoriously difficult to pin down irrespective of encompasses enriched in a myriad of ways engagement with the arts interpreting the world around us
