Task 4 — Retell, then take a line (orally)

Speaking only on this page. In about two minutes: (1) Summarise the five moves of the text — what each paragraph does. (2) Say which criticism you sympathise with more: the space for work, the money, or the author’s defence — and why, in a couple of clear sentences. Re-use signposts from Task 2 and chunks from Task 3 if you can.

Сначала пересказ по абзацам, потом своё отношение. Писать ничего не надо. Английский, русский или смесь — как вам в группе договоритесь.

Paragraph jobs (loose map)

  • (1) Why the building mattered, how bad it had got, and that the project is now complete.
  • (2) Aesthetic success inside + kids’ area; outside kept, new dome, mixed reactions.
  • (3) The “but” on function: looks vs space for people who depend on the library to work.
  • (4) The money fight + the author’s rebuttal (libraries = vital, if intangibly).
  • (5) A wider policy finish: if money were unlimited vs real constraints and stark choices.
