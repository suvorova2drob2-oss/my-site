Task 3 — Retell the review (speaking, not writing)

There is no text box for a written essay on this page. The idea is to say the recap out loud, in English or Russian or mixed — as if you are explaining the review to a friend, using your own words and (if you like) a few of the “strong” English phrases.

Задание: устно пересказать суть: что за выставка, в чём претензия к работам, к курации, к обещаниям в рекламе, куда вас посылают в конце. Писать «по смыслу» через экран не обязательно — говорим вслух, можно записать голос в заметки в телефоне при желании.

Cover these points in order (roughly)

  • where you were, what the opening was like, first impression (underwhelming?)
  • how you rate the works and what was wrong with technique / originality
  • why the layout by artist broke any thematic story (curation issue)
  • what the promo promised (insight, heritage) vs what you actually felt when you left
  • where you send readers instead and on what terms (contrast: another gallery’s standards)

Phrases you may drop in (if they fit your speech)

immediately struck by underwhelming at best technical prowess glaring errors nigh on impossible overriding thematic connection curation give insight into national heritage disillusioned in terms of professionalism flawless (contrast)
