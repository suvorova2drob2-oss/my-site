From the book (Ex. 6) — which adjectives are mostly positive (+) or negative (−)? (click to show)

Rough guide: (+) formidable, profound, flawless (often “good” in reviews). (−) appalling, dubious, crude, disillusioned, mediocre (critic’s tone, doubt, or person feels let down). Context can shift a word — this is a first pass, not a law.
